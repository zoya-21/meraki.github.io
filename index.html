ABOUT MERAKI

WRITE TO PLEASE YOUR SOUL

We all have gone through the times where we had words to speak, but we remained Silent.We had emotions to express, but we remained Numb.We never let them come out of us,and over the time they become a part of us our personalities our thoughts at 3:00 a.m. They still bother us from time to time....But why are we letting our own thoughts bother us so much? Why are we not letting it come out without the fear of being judged or mocked or rejected? Let them come out freely,without any fear. Write it,Speak it,Express it freely to please your soul!!

Meraki is a website which lets you put something of yourself into work. It comprises a small collection of my poems and proses which I have penned down to make myself feel free. And even you can feel free to share your writeups with me on my email id.
